The Three Core Pillars of Digital India 2047

1. Digital Infrastructure as a Core Utility

Ensuring high-speed internet, secure data centers, and universal connectivity across rural and urban landscapes, making digital resources as fundamental as water and electricity.

2. Governance and Services On Demand

Revolutionizing public services through AI, blockchain, and cloud technologies for seamless, transparent, and efficient delivery, accessible from any device, anywhere, anytime.

3. Digital Empowerment of Citizens

Achieving universal digital literacy, fostering a culture of cybersecurity awareness, and enabling citizens to actively participate in the digital economy and decision-making processes.

Next-Gen Innovations Driving Digital India

Artificial Intelligence & ML
